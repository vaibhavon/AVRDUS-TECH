Laser scanning uses the controlled deflection of a steered laser beam to measure the distance between objects. These rapidly acquired measurements are used to create digital three-dimensional (3D) models of objects such as buildings, landscapes, and other environments. Surveying allows you to precisely determine the coordinates and position of an object on the earth. 3D laser scanning and surveying projects require specialized expertise and knowledge of state-of-the-art equipment, dimension control and land surveying practices. High-definition 3D digital capture technology minimizes business disruption and reduces risk by capturing complete as-built data of construction work on-site without disrupting operations.


Why should you rent a 3D scanner and surveying equipment?


Purchasing a 3D laser scanning device is a large capital investment. Our rental service will help you save cash and ensure you have the right equipment for your project.
