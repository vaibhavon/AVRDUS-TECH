In modern times, the importance and necessity of preserving cultural heritage and archaeological sites has increased tremendously. In particular, sites of artistic or historical value require special attention, as well as high-precision techniques and comprehensive and regular documentation. We provide advanced techniques for the precise measurement and recording of cultural relics without damaging the original structure or surroundings of the relics. Our solution provide a very simple and convenient way to accurately capture, document, and share spatial information of cultural heritage through various platforms.

This data can be made available to a wide range of users for research and other purposes. We use advanced laser scanners to create point clouds of scanned ruins and monuments to capture accurate 3D information that can further create an exact layout of site and can be easily documented as 3D CAD models to create digital twin.

Benefits of Using 3D Laser Scanners for Heritage Preservation:

  • Easy to Use
  • Fast and Reliable Creation of Virtual Copies
  • Highly Accurate Information and 3D Documentation
